# Product

## Users

Technical operators who configure and monitor Masumi payment infrastructure. The product is optimized for speed and clarity: operators need to understand wallet purpose, funding state, and automated behavior quickly while managing live Cardano payment sources.

## Product Purpose

Masumi Payment Service provides an administrative interface for creating and operating payment-source wallets, registering agents, monitoring transactions, and maintaining reliable settlement. Success means operators can configure the correct wallet type confidently, see which automation applies to it, and diagnose low-balance conditions before they interrupt payments.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and tool-like. The interface should match the existing dark UI and feel trustworthy and focused, with enough context to prevent mistakes without slowing down experienced operators.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative crypto and fintech visuals, speculative-finance styling, excessive card grids, neon accents, and visually novel controls for standard administrative tasks. Avoid exposing capabilities that do not apply to the selected wallet type.

## Design Principles

1. Make wallet purpose and capability boundaries explicit before creation.
2. Adapt the workflow to the operator's current context while keeping type switching available.
3. Prefer one coherent creation flow over parallel actions that compete for attention.
4. Surface automation where it is configured, and hide impossible or unsafe combinations.
5. Keep operational detail legible without making common actions feel dense.
6. Let the All tab open the wallet-type picker; Purchasing, Selling, and Funding tabs preselect their type while preserving switching.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and complete keyboard behavior. Preserve visible focus states, semantic labels, reduced-motion preferences, restrained motion, and status communication that does not rely on color alone.
